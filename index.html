Meet the Gardener

Vincent Ndlovu

An informal interview was conducted with Vincent, a gardener at Belgium Campus iTversity, West Campus, to gain insight into his role and daily responsibilities.

Vincent explained that his primary duties include maintaining the campus gardens, watering plants, trimming vegetation, and ensuring that outdoor areas remain clean and well-presented. He is also responsible for cleaning and maintaining the pool area, ensuring it is safe and hygienic for use.

He highlighted that his work contributes to creating a comfortable and visually appealing environment for both students and staff. He also acknowledged that challenges such as weather conditions and managing large outdoor areas can make the job demanding — but he meets them with the same level of dedication every day.

"A clean and well-kept place makes people feel welcome, and I'm proud to be part of that."

— Vincent Ndlovu · Campus Gardener, Belgium Campus iTversity
