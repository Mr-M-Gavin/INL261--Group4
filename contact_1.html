About Vincent's Role

An informal interview with Vincent revealed the depth of his commitment. His primary duties include maintaining the campus gardens, watering plants, trimming vegetation, and ensuring outdoor areas remain clean and well-presented. He is also responsible for cleaning and maintaining the pool area. Vincent's work creates a comfortable environment for the entire Belgium Campus community — a contribution that often goes unseen.
