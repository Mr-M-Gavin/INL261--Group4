What Vincent Does

Services & Responsibilities

Vincent Ndlovu, Campus Gardener at Belgium Campus iTversity, is responsible for keeping the West Campus grounds safe, clean, and beautiful every day. Hover a card to see what each service involves.

🌱 Daily Duty

Garden Maintenance

Keeping the campus gardens healthy, clean, and beautiful every single day.

What this involves

  • Daily watering of all plants and flower beds
  • Trimming and edging vegetation
  • Pruning shrubs and trees
  • Planting seasonal flowers
  • Removing weeds and keeping areas tidy
💧 Specialist Task

Pool Management

Cleaning and maintaining the campus pool so it remains safe and hygienic for use.

What this involves

  • Daily cleaning and skimming
  • Chemical and pH balancing
  • Filter and pump inspections
  • Safety compliance checks
  • Seasonal pool preparation
🏡 Ongoing

Campus Beautification

Creating a comfortable and visually appealing environment for students and staff.

What this involves

  • Pathway and entrance upkeep
  • Seasonal plant displays
  • Outdoor furniture cleaning
  • Signage surrounds tidying
  • Event space preparation
🛡️ Priority

Safety & Cleanliness

Ensuring all outdoor spaces are hazard-free and safe for everyone on campus.

What this involves

  • Removing trip hazards daily
  • Clearing leaves and debris
  • Waste removal and sorting
  • Reporting maintenance faults
  • Wet surface hazard marking
🔧 General

Outdoor Maintenance

General upkeep of all outdoor infrastructure and common areas across the West Campus.

What this involves

  • Mowing and lawn edging
  • Keeping walkways clear
  • Maintaining outdoor furniture
  • Checking irrigation systems
  • Reporting structural issues
💪 Work Ethic

Dedication & Consistency

Showing up every day — Mon–Sat — with the same level of care and commitment.

In Vincent's words

  • Working Mon–Fri: 06:00–16:00
  • Working Sat: 07:00–12:00
  • Managing large outdoor areas daily
  • Adapting to weather challenges
  • "My job requires dedication and consistency every day."

From the Interview

About Vincent's Work

An informal interview was conducted with Vincent, a gardener at Belgium Campus iTversity, West Campus, to gain insight into his role and daily responsibilities.

Vincent explained that his primary duties include maintaining the campus gardens, watering plants, trimming vegetation, and ensuring that outdoor areas remain clean and well-presented. He is also responsible for cleaning and maintaining the pool area, ensuring it is safe and hygienic for use.

"I take pride in my work because I know the environment affects how people feel every day." — Vincent Ndlovu · Campus Gardener, Belgium Campus iTversity

He also mentioned that challenges such as weather conditions and managing large areas can make the job demanding. Despite this, his commitment never wavers.

"My job may seem simple, but it requires dedication and consistency every day." — Vincent Ndlovu · Campus Gardener, Belgium Campus iTversity
